Our Cleaning Services

Deep Cleaning

Our Deep Cleaning Service is designed to give your home a fresh start by tackling both the visible and hidden dirt. We thoroughly clean all common areas, including bedrooms, kitchens, and bathrooms. This includes wiping and dusting surfaces, sweeping and mopping floors, vacuuming carpets, and cleaning mirrors, door handles, and light switches. We also focus on the areas that are often overlooked or hard to reach. This includes cleaning behind appliances (like the fridge and oven), baseboards, door frames, interior windows, blinds, ceiling fans, light fixtures, wall spots, and grout. High-touch areas are sanitized to help reduce the spread of germs. Our deep cleaning ensures your home is not only clean but also feels refreshed and comfortable, making it an ideal choice for a detailed reset of your space.

Standard Cleaning

Our Standard Cleaning Service ensures a thorough cleaning of all common areas, including bedrooms, kitchens, and bathrooms. We wipe and dust surfaces, sweep and mop floors and baseboards, and vacuum carpets. Additionally, we clean mirrors, door handles, light switches, and kitchen cabinet fronts, addressing even the finer details. High-touch areas are given extra attention to reduce the spread of germs. This service is ideal for maintaining a fresh, clean, and healthy living environment.

Post-Construction Cleaning

Our Post-Construction Cleaning Service is designed to handle the mess left behind after construction or renovation projects. We ensure your property is spotless, safe, and move-in ready by tackling dust, debris, and residue with precision. This service includes removing construction dust and debris, deep cleaning all surfaces, windows, fixtures, and hard-to-reach areas. Whether it’s a newly built space or a renovated one, our cleaning ensures your property is polished, refreshed, and perfectly prepared for its next use.

Standard Moving Cleaning

Our Move-In/Out Standard Cleaning Service is perfect for homes that need a general refresh during a move. This service focuses on all common areas, bedrooms, kitchens, and bathrooms. We wipe and dust surfaces, sweep and mop floors, vacuum carpets, and clean mirrors, door handles, light switches, and kitchen cabinet fronts. High-touch areas receive extra care to help reduce the spread of germs. This service is ideal for homes in good condition that need a light touch-up before moving out or settling into your new space.

Deep Moving Cleaning

Our Move-In/Out Deep Cleaning Service is designed for a comprehensive and detailed clean, ensuring every corner of your home is spotless and refreshed. This service includes all the tasks in our Moving Standard Cleaning Service, plus cleaning behind appliances (such as the fridge and oven), baseboards, door frames, interior windows, blinds, ceiling fans, light fixtures, wall spots, and grout. High-touch areas are sanitized to minimize the spread of germs. This service is perfect for homes requiring a thorough reset, whether you’re moving into a new space or leaving your old one in pristine condition.
