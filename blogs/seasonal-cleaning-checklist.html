Seasonal Cleaning Checklist: Keep Your Home Fresh All Year Round

Published on December 3, 2024

Maintaining a clean and organized home requires regular attention, especially as the seasons change. Our seasonal cleaning checklist ensures that your home stays fresh, comfortable, and well-maintained throughout the year.

Spring Cleaning

  • Deep clean windows and screens
  • Organize closets and donate unused clothing
  • Clean and inspect HVAC systems
  • Wash curtains and blinds
  • Declutter outdoor areas and prepare gardens

Summer Maintenance

  • Clean and seal grout in bathrooms and kitchens
  • Maintain outdoor furniture and grills
  • Inspect and clean gutters
  • Check and repair window screens
  • Deep clean carpets and rugs

Fall Preparation

  • Clean and store summer items
  • Prepare fireplaces and chimneys
  • Inspect insulation and weatherproof windows
  • Organize pantry and check for expired items
  • Decorate for the upcoming holiday season

Winter Upkeep

  • Maintain heating systems and replace filters
  • Protect pipes from freezing
  • Organize storage areas and basements
  • Clean and sanitize bathrooms thoroughly
  • Plan for holiday cleaning and hosting
